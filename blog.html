    A show I had completely forgotten about was on the TV a few days ago, and it's a wild ride. The Odyssey (1992) tells the story of Jay, a kid who fell in a coma and gets transported to a post-apocalyptic alternate reality called the Downworld, while his mom and friends try to wake him up in the Upworld. I have fond memories of watching this show when I was a kid so I rewatched the whole thing and I can definitely say that they don't make shows like this anymore. Contrary to popular belief, the show does not take place inside Jay's head. We get clues throughout the series that the Downworld is some sort of parallel universe:

    • Medea, a character from the Downworld, is shown as another coma patient in the Upworld
    • Donna, Jay's best friend, dreams of Jay in the Downworld
    • Jay's dad seems to have vanished in the Downworld himself years ago
    • The Downworld has a structure and hierarchy with clubs and factions that are independent of Jay
